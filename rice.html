Rice

What is riceRice is easily grown over the globe and its cooking process is normally not complicated cause it consists of a few ingredients we will talk about

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • Salt to taste
  • oil

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  3. Add the rinsed rice and a pinch of salt to the boiling water.
  4. Reduce heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for about 18-20 minutes or until the rice is tender and the water is absorbed.
  5. Remove from heat and let it sit covered for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Turn the rice around and serve while hot or to your preference
